Dr Dan White is currently the Executive Director of Catholic Schools for the Archdiocese of Sydney.

Prior to his appointment Dr White was the Director of Catholic Education for the Archdiocese of Hobart for six years. He previously served in senior leadership roles in the Dioceses of Parramatta and Bathurst.
